About

I am a M.S. student at the University of Science and Technology (UST), affiliated with the Korea Institute of Machinery and Materials (KIMM) School, where I began my graduate studies in March 2025. I received my B.S. in Energy and Electrical Engineering from Korea University of Technology and Education (TUKOREA) in February 2025. My research focuses on the optimization and development of renewable energy systems, particularly photovoltaic (PV) power generation systems including system design, performance monitoring, and grid integration analysis.
